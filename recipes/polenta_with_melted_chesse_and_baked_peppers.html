Polenta with melted chesse and baked peppers

Description

Reading the title of this meal fiils you with intrigue. How can they mix?

How could they taste?

Well, I tought just the same, but when my girlfriend made me an "topcit" from her home region of Harghita, I was filled with joy, the nuances in the aroma really stick with you.

So, if you are interested in some exotic Transilvanian dish, I highly recommend you Topcit!

Ingredients:

  • Corn flour
  • Marinated baked peppers
  • Homemade chesse

Steps:

  1. Melt the chesse in a pan
  2. Boil the corn flour in a pot
  3. Drop the baked peppers in the pan with the molten chesse
  4. Flip the polenta on a hard surface
  5. Enjoy Topcit!
