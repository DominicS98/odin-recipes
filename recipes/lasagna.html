Lasagna

Description

Everybody knows and loves Garfield! That fat little orange cat with his sharp humor.

But ever wondered what made Garfield that fat?

Well that wonderfull mix of chesse and spiced meat!

It's not as hard to cook as it may seem, just follow the steps below and become a fat tomcat!

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced meat
  • 250 g of chesse
  • 250 ml milk
  • Lasagna dought slices
  • and a mistery lasagna herbs pack!

Steps:

  • Mix the milk with the herbs
  • Fry the minced meat
  • Mix the meat with the liquid
  • Make layers of lasagna mix and the dough in a tray
  • Put in oven for 45 min
  • Enjoy!
